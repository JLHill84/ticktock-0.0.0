Directors RENEWAL REGISTRATION - $45.00

 

 

 

To renew your credential you will need the following:

 

_____60 clock hours of training or 6 CEU's of training within the last 2 years.

++++++ If you are short on hours, we have several for you to choose from on our Management Training page.

 

_____You must have a current CPR CARD.

 

_____Your current Directors Credential can not be expired past 30 days.  If it is expired past 30 days you must re-take the Directors Credential course again and apply as a new Director with Child Care Licensing.  

 

_____Must currently work in a licensed child care facility or have 80 hours of experience working with young children in the last 2 years.  

 

Register and pay for the renewal $45.00 by clicking the application button below.  You will be directed to a secure payment site that will take your registration information and payment.  Once payment is received you will be sent, via the email you provide - instructions on how to log in and complete the renewal process.
