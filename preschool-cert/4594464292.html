The PRESCHOOL (Lead Teacher Credential) is a national credential that enables the credential holder to work as a lead teacher in a preschool classroom.  This course meets the state requirements for preschool certification and can be used and is accepted at the Elementary School level.  

 

Course topics:  Safety, Healthy, Learning Environments, Curriculum Development, Discipline and Guidance, Families and Professionalism.  

 

Clock Hours: 30 clock hours.

 

The student must be 18 years of age or older and have a high school diploma or G.E.D.  

 

Experience in the childcare field is helpful but NOT required.  

 

The course is 100% online and can be completed in as little as 4 weeks however, the student will have a full 90 days to complete the program.  

 

Class can also be used to meet the preservice requirement.

 

Certification must be renewed every 2 years.
